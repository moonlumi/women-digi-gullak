National Pension Scheme

Launched by the Government of India and administered by the Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Authority (PFRDA), National Pension Scheme can be considered as one of the best government-backed savings schemes for ladies. All the women investors willing to have a decent financial corpus after their retirement and who do not will to take major financial risks in life should blindly opt for the National Pension Scheme option. With 2 types of NPS accounts to choose from, that is, Tier-I and Tier-II, NPS is considered a viable savings scheme for the ladies.
